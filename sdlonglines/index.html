About This Webpage:

This website is dedicated to the preserving and the recognition of the old Bell system of microwave towers accross South Dakota. Long Lines towers as they are called provided long distance telephone and TV communications across the United States by relaying from tower to tower. These towers are now long defunct and have either been removed, abandoned, or converted for other uses. Today, many of these towers have been converted to cellular service and many of the old horn antennas on the towers still lay intact. This website contains many images of some of the long lines towers across South Dakota and other states. Take a look!
