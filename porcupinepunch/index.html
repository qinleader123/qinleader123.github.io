About KFØCCH

I took my tech operator's class ham test on October 13th, 2020 and passed. On October 21st, I was added to the FCC Database as KFØCCH. I already had a radio at that point and rougly a week later, my first contact was made with my callsign.

What's on this website?

This site contains my logs, my radio projects as well as many helpful resources for amateur radio operators.

I am based in Brookings, SD. so usually whenever I am talking about events or lovcations in "Our area" I am usually talking about Brookings and it's adjacent counties. However, regardless of who you are, where you are from, what you do, or even if you an amateur operator or not, you are welcome to visit this site.
