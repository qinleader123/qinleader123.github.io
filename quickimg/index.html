Bulletin Board

How To Get On The Board

Just make a GIF or image that is 200x100 and provide a link (if you want). You can make it other resolutions, just make sure it can be scaled to 200x100. It doesn't even have to be an advertisement; it could be a GIF of your cat if you want. Where does my image go on the board? Everywhere you see a "Your Ad Can Go Here" GIF is just an empty slot for someone else to fill. Just pick one of them to put your ad in.
