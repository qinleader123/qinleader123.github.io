Mood

NOTE: all quotes are direct quotes from the story.

The mood is mysterious. Evidence: (Narrator) "the night was cold and wet, but in the small parlour of Laburnam Villa..." (Sergeant Morris) "it had a spell put on it by an old fakir" the mood is created by the weather and the mysteriousness of the monkey's paw.
